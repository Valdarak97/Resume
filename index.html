The past

Before being interested in programming

I was not the most diligent student in the past, when it came to studying. This was not true when it came to gaming. When playing I would pay attention to the smallest details, would devise strategies. Later I applied this to studying, but more on that later. I loved hanging out with friends, playing football together, talking about sports. I was not very active in the past regarding sports (disregarding the occasional football match). I was always interested in programming when I was little, but didn't have the motivation to start learning it. The main disadvantage in school was that I was in a humanities class. We didn't learn programming at all. I also only learned math until the 10th grade. After graduating I started looking at my options regarding universities, so I joined the only viable option for me, which was the Faculty of Letters.

This did not last long (aprox. 6 months), because learning there did not motivate me at all. After this I have worked in 2 storages, so I could earn some money, and help my parents. The next bit was the turning point in my life. Realising that I didn't want to spend my entire life working something that I didn't enjoy, I started thinking about my options. After much deliberation I decided the I will start learning programming and apply to the Faculy of Mathemtics and Computer Science in my hometown of Cluj-Napoca, Romania. The hard part was learning 4 years' worth of mathematics and programming in one year. The next year was hard, but extremely rewarding. I learned mathematics with the help of a private tutor, and I learned C++, with the help of the book written by Bjarne Stroustrup (creator and developer of C++) for beginners.
